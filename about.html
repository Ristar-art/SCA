Welcome to
Soweto Chef’s Academy

Soweto Chef’s Academy (Pty) Ltd. is the first culinary school of its kind in Soweto, conveniently located at Walter Sisulu Square in Kliptown. We are committed to delivering quality training that enhances your skills in the latest trends of hospitality, and our strong suit lies in providing valuable work placement experiences.

Our mission is to offer the finest professional culinary education available. Students choose Soweto Chef’s Academy (Pty) Ltd. for our exceptional educational programs and the outstanding career opportunities they provide.

Our programs cater to individuals with a passion for food, whether they are aspiring chefs aiming for culinary stardom, professionals seeking to advance their skills, or food enthusiasts looking to elevate their home cooking.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide you with the best professional culinary education. Students come to Soweto Chef’s Academy (Pty) Ltd. for our gold-standard education programs and outstanding career opportunities.

  • Our goal is to deliver top-quality culinary education.
  • We aim to offer exceptional career prospects to our students.
  • We strive to maintain the highest standards in culinary training.

National Certificate and Skills Programs

Soweto Chef’s Academy (Pty) Ltd. offers nationally recognized certificates and skills programs that pave the way for an exciting career in the culinary world. Our programs include associate qualifications in culinary arts, baking and pastry arts, applied food studies, and food and beverage studies.

Graduates of Soweto Chef’s Academy (Pty) Ltd. are equipped to pursue diverse career paths, such as becoming successful chefs, restaurant owners, managers, entrepreneurs, food stylists, food writers, educators, and more. Invest in your future with an education from Soweto Chef’s Academy (Pty) Ltd. and obtain the credentials you need for a fulfilling career in the food industry.

Food Enthusiast Programs (Non – Accredited Courses)

These programs are designed for individuals seeking to enhance their cooking skills, whether for home-cooked meals or baking. We offer a range of courses conducted in professionally equipped kitchens, with expert instruction and access to a wealth of resources including cookbooks, DVDs, and culinary equipment in our practical laboratory. Join us at Soweto Chef’s Academy (Pty) Ltd. and make it your culinary playground.

Soweto Chef’s Academy (Pty) Ltd. also provides hands-on custom programming for groups of 30 or more, tailored to meet the professional development needs of the food, beverage, and hospitality industry. Our team collaborates with yours to create program curricula that align with your specific needs and goals.
